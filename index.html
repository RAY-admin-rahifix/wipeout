About Us

We are a group of six 6th graders who decided to start a car washing business together. We wanted to earn our own money, learn responsibility, and work as a team.

Our goal is to provide good, reliable car washes while being friendly and hardworking. We all help each other to make sure every car is clean and looks great when we're finished.

We started this business to gain experience and show that 6th graders can work together to run a small business. Thank you for supporting us!
