About Us

We are a group of dedicated 6th graders who started a car washing business to earn money and learn responsibility. We take pride in our work and make sure every car looks amazing!

Our goal is to provide quality service, work as a team, and show that young people can run a successful business. Every team member puts in 100% effort to make customers happy.

We started this business to gain real-world experience and prove that 6th graders can do professional work. Thank you for supporting us!
